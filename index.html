Chinese America Community Hand-in-Hand
Founded during the Covid-19 Pandemic crisis, CAC Hand-in-Hand (Chinese American Community Hand-In-Hand) is a non-profit public interest community organization which is dedicated to safe guard our frontline health care professionals. The CAC's mission is to call upon every Chinese American and the general public residing in USA to get united, and support our front line health care professionals to survive this unprecedented crisis.
